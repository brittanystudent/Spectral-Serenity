Spirit Soak Bath Ritual

A luxurious soak in a warm bath infused with herbs, salts, and essential oils known for their calming properties. The treatment includes beastly bath bombs that fizz with eerie colors and scents, creating an immersive experience that relaxes both body and mind.

Ectoplasm Exfoliation Scrub

A sugar and salt scrub infused with ghostly fragrances like “Villainous Vanilla” and “Surreal Citrus.” As you’re gently scrubbed, the exfoliation reveals softer skin while the scents transport you to a spectral realm.

Zombie Detox Foot Ritual

A revitalizing foot treatment that begins with a warm foot soak in a bubbling cauldron of herbal-infused water. Followed by an exfoliating scrub and a soothing massage, this treatment is designed to relieve the fatigue of wandering the afterlife, leaving your feet feeling refreshed and alive!

Macabre Mud Mask

This treatment uses a rich, mineral-packed mud from the depths of a haunted lake. Applied all over the body, the mask draws out impurities and nourishes the skin, leaving you feeling rejuvenated—just like rising from a grave!
