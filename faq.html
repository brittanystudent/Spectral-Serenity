Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a haunted spa?

A haunted spa offers a unique blend of relaxation and supernatural themes, combining traditional spa treatments with eerie ambiance and spooky elements for a memorable experience.

2. Are the treatments safe?

Yes! All treatments are designed with your safety and comfort in mind. Our trained staff ensures that every service is both relaxing and enjoyable.

3. Do I need to make a reservation?

Yes, we recommend making reservations in advance, especially around Halloween or during peak times, to ensure you get your preferred treatments and times.

4. What should I wear?

We provide robes and slippers for your comfort. You can arrive in comfortable clothing and change at the spa. If you’re participating in themed treatments, feel free to embrace the spirit with fun attire!

5. Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely! We encourage group visits. Just be sure to book your appointments together for a shared experience.

6. Are there any age restrictions?

Guests must be 18 or older to receive most treatments. We do offer special family-friendly events on certain occasions, so check our schedule!

7. What if I’m scared of ghosts?

While we embrace a spooky theme, the experience is designed to be fun and light-hearted. You can enjoy the treatments without engaging in any ghostly narratives if you prefer!

8. What are the payment options?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and cash. Gift cards are also available for purchase.

9. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, but please give us at least 24 hours' notice to avoid cancellation fees. We’ll be happy to help you reschedule your visit!

10. What if I have allergies or medical conditions?

Please inform us of any allergies or medical conditions before your appointment so we can tailor your treatments accordingly.



If you have any other questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our team—we’re here to ensure your Spectral spa experience is unforgettable!


At Spectral Serenity, our purpose is to create a sanctuary where spirits—both living and beyond—can find solace, rejuvenation, and connection. We believe in the power of self-care to transcend the physical realm, offering a unique space for relaxation and healing. Our treatments are designed to harmonize the energies of the body and spirit, allowing each guest to release burdens and embrace their true essence.
