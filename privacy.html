Privacy Policy for Spectral Serenity

Effective Date 10/31/2024

At Spectral Serenity, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our spa or interact with our services. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide (both for current life or past lives) when booking services, signing up for our newsletter, or contacting us.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we collect payment details, which are processed securely through our third-party payment processors. Souls are processed by Afterlife Accounts and are subject to their terms of service.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use our website and services, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.


2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services, including bookings and appointments.
  • To communicate with you about your appointments, special offers, and promotions.
  • To improve our services and enhance your experience at our spa.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.


3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our spa, processing payments, or conducting our business.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.


4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.


5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to object to or restrict the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.


6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
